My Music

🎵 "Cowboys and Sad Songs"

Every cowboy has a story to tell, and mine comes with a melody. A heartfelt tribute to the men who ride the range and the songs that keep them company.

🎵 "When the Prairie Calls Me Home"

There's something about wide open spaces that calls to the soul. A longing ballad about the pull of home and the land that shapes us.

🎵 "Where the River Forgets My Name"

Sometimes we need to go where nobody knows who we used to be. A haunting melody about finding peace in solitude and letting the current wash away our past.

🎵 "Dancing Through My Heart"

Love has a way of moving through you like music itself. A tender ballad about how the right person can make your whole world feel like a slow dance under the stars.

🎵 "A Cowboy's Life"

Wide open ranges, long days in the saddle, and the freedom that comes with living on your own terms. A tribute to the timeless spirit of the American cowboy.

🎵 "My Truck Left Me and Took My Dog"

When everything goes wrong, it really goes wrong. A tongue-in-cheek country anthem about life's cruel sense of humor and the things we hold dear.

About David

🎸

Welcome to my musical journey! I'm David van Coolwijk, a country music artist who believes in the power of authentic storytelling combined with innovative technology.

My music blends traditional country songwriting with AI performance technology, creating something both timeless and cutting-edge. Each song tells a story - from prairie landscapes to cowboy tales, from heartbreak to hope.

When I'm not writing songs, you'll find me exploring the intersection of music and technology, always searching for new ways to connect with audiences through the universal language of country music.
