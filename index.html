About David

🎸

Welcome to my musical journey! I'm David van Coolwijk, a country music artist who believes in the power of authentic storytelling combined with innovative technology.

My music blends traditional country songwriting with AI performance technology, creating something both timeless and cutting-edge. Each song tells a story - from prairie landscapes to cowboy tales, from heartbreak to hope.

When I'm not writing songs, you'll find me exploring the intersection of music and technology, always searching for new ways to connect with audiences through the universal language of country music.
